About Me

Incase you were curious to who built this site, that would be me, Trey Hope. I am a 22 year old graphic desinger, mobile applications developer, and web-designer from Dayton, Ohio.

I have had previous work experience in relation to the majority of my skill sets, including companies like Toyota Boshoku of America, Seimens PLM Software, and Tata Consultancy Services. I have also worked as a Web Developer for both London Computer Systems and Northern Kentucky University as well.

Most of my work revolves around user graphics and mobile media for businesses, promotional use, various organizations, and anyone who enjoys such material. If you're interested in my work, be sure to send me a request!
